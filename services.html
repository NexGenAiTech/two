Project timelines vary based on complexity. A basic website takes 2-3 weeks, web applications 4-8 weeks, and complex AI projects 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines after requirement analysis.

Yes, we offer maintenance packages starting from ₹5,000/month. This includes updates, security patches, backups, and 24/7 monitoring. Custom packages are available based on your needs.

We work with modern technologies including React, Node.js, Python, TensorFlow, Flutter, AWS, and more. We choose the best stack for your specific project requirements.

Absolutely! We can analyze, improve, and extend your existing codebase. We also offer code audits and refactoring services to improve performance and maintainability.
